Woman, Water, Wild

Woman, Water, Wild is a blog for women interested in the pursuit of outdoor water adventures including kayaking, fishing, boating, wakeboarding and more. For those unsure where to begin or those interested in expanding their range of water-related activities, Woman, Water, Wild provides guidelines, techniques and how-to tips specifically catered to meeting the unique needs of the female water adventurer. This blog will give step-by-step instructions for each adventure its host introduces on the site in addition to packing lists that consider the needs of women, anecdotes from which to learn what not to do, and helpful hints to enjoy the water while being safe and having fun.
